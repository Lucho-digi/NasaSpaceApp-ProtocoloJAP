About Raincheck

Raincheck is a modern and minimalist weather application, designed to offer accurate weather forecasts with a clean and intuitive interface. This project was developed for the Nasa Space Apps Challenge.

The goal is to provide a useful and aesthetically pleasing tool to plan your daily, weekly, or even time-specific activities, taking weather conditions into account.

Main Features

  • 100% Vanilla JS: No external frameworks or libraries for the main logic.
  • Responsive Design: Works perfectly on desktops, tablets, and mobile.
  • Light/Dark Theme: Switch between modes for your visual comfort.
  • Interactive Map: Easily select locations with Leaflet.js.
  • Modern CSS: Structured with variables for easy customization.
